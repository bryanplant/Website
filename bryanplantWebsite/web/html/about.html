I enjoy hiking, camping, playing soccer, and snowboarding in the winter. I also like to play guitar, video games and traveling internationally whenever I have the opportunity.

    I especially have a passion for all things computer related. Since before high school I've experimented with coding with everything from TI calculators to Lego Mindstorms and Arduino micro controllers. I am always coming up with new ideas for what I want to do with code, like this website for example. The animation of this website is coded with Dart which I had never used before and uses a simple genetic algorithm which is also new to me. This and all of my other personal projects can be found at my Github

    While I enjoy my classes and working on personal projects I am most interested in obtaining real world experience outside of the classroom. I think that working on real world problems forces a new style of learning and I am always willing to learn and adapt to new situations. Thanks for checking out my website!
